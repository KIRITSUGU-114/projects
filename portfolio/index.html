Tech: WordPress, Elementor PRO

My role in this project

I designed this online store in Figma based on my client's needs, typography, colors, custom features, etc. Once the design was finished, I used the CMS Wordpress and Elementor PRO to build and style it, I also used CSS to give it a custom style that I couldn't achieve with the theme's own settings, then using the Woocommerce plugin I put the online store into operation by adding and optimizing products for SEO, I also optimized the content of the pillar pages for search engines and the site assets for accelerated loading, it currently ranks #1 - #5 in search results related to market keywords.

In addition to making the necessary DNS configurations to connect the previously purchased domain with the Hosting that was not from the same provider, I was in charge of creating the corporate emails and the necessary configurations to make the connection with all the company's devices.

Once the project was completed, I also participated in the collection and analysis of Google Analytics and WooCommerce data for marketing strategies. I have also worked frequently with the owner of the company to carry out redesigns, updates and optimizations of content, adding new features and maintenance.

About me

Who am I and where do I come from?

My name, you probably already know it. I'm 23 years old and I'm a dreamer looking for my next challenge. I was born, raised and currently live in a small town called Esquipulas located in Guatemala. I started creating websites as an attempt to discover what to do with my life. At first they were super simple and to be honest, quite ugly, but as I got more and more into this world, I fell in love with it more. That's right, I started to discover what to do with my life and I hit the nail on the head, I fell in love with software development.

My current goals

Currently my main goal is to continue learning, improve my skills, move forward and create to be a better developer every day. I want to continue creating accessible, beautiful, adaptable and optimal web experiences.
