My work

Luces Guatemala

October 2022 - May 2023 Designer / Developer / SEO

Online store that works as a second sales channel for the company Luces Guatemala. Responsive design for multiple devices, optimized for search results and accelerated loading.

Tech: Wordpress, Elementor PRO Show more add Show less remove
My role in this project

I designed this online store in Figma based on my client's needs, typography, colors, custom features, etc. Once the design was finished, I used the CMS Wordpress and Elementor PRO to build and style it, I also used CSS3 to give it a custom style that I couldn't achieve with the theme's own settings, then using the Woocommerce plugin I put the online store into operation by adding and optimizing products for SEO, I also optimized the content of the pillar pages for search engines and the site assets for accelerated loading, it currently ranks #1 - #5 in search results related to market keywords.

In addition to making the necessary DNS configurations to connect the previously purchased domain with the Hosting that was not from the same provider, I was in charge of creating the corporate emails and the necessary configurations to make the connection with all the company's devices.

After the project was finished, I have also participated in the collection and analysis of Google Analytics and WooCommerce data for marketing strategies.
